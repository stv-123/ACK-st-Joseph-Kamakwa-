Brief History

ACK St Joseph Kamakwa Parish is part of the Anglican Church of Kenya under the Mt Kenya West Diocese. Located in Nyeri County, the parish comprises two churches: the main church in Kamakwa, and another in Kiriti. The parish serves both spiritual and community roles through worship, education, and outreach ministries.
